About
Dozy Mmobuosi

Dozy Mmobuosi is a globally recognized Nigerian entrepreneur, technology innovator, and philanthropist with a deep-rooted commitment to advancing Africa’s economic and social development. As the founder of several high-impact ventures spanning fintech, agriculture, AI, education, and infrastructure, Dozy’s mission is clear: to build a prosperous and self-reliant Africa through sustainable investments and inclusive innovation.

Dozy has spearheaded numerous initiatives that contribute meaningfully to African development, particularly in Nigeria and Malawi. He has led investments in scalable technology platforms and infrastructure projects that empower local communities, create jobs, and foster digital transformation across underserved regions. In Nigeria, Dozy has built and supported ventures focused on AI-powered education, data infrastructure, and healthcare innovation. In Malawi, his philanthropic outreach has centered on healthcare and capacity building for rural populations, expanding access to basic services and life-saving interventions.

Advancing Healthcare through the Dozy Mmobuosi Foundation

Founded with a mission to improve lives across Africa, the Dozy Mmobuosi Foundation (DMF) is at the forefront of tackling some of the continent’s most pressing healthcare challenges. One of its flagship programs is the Prostate Cancer Awareness and Screening Initiative, launched to address the alarming rise in prostate cancer cases among African men. The initiative has delivered free prostate cancer screenings and health education to thousands of men across Nigeria and other African nations. DMF is also committed to healthcare access, nutrition, and public health awareness, particularly in underserved rural communities.

Philanthropy

Mmobuosi is deeply committed to supporting communities that lack access to opportunities. His philanthropic efforts focus on bridging gaps in education, healthcare, and technology access, ensuring that underserved populations have the resources needed to thrive in today’s fast-evolving world. His initiatives support major educational institutions, social justice organizations, the arts, and scientific research for diseases such as breast and prostate cancer.

To address healthcare disparities, Mmobuosi launched a mobile prostate cancer screening service, offering free medical check-ups to men across Nigeria. This initiative underscores his commitment to preventive healthcare and raising awareness about critical health issues

His philanthropic vision is embodied in The Dozy Mmobuosi Foundation, an organization dedicated to fostering human development in Africa, particularly in rural regions. The foundation focuses on healthcare, education, entrepreneurship, technology skills, and partnership development. Its mission is to promote equity in healthcare access, enhance educational opportunities, and support technological advancement for economic growth. Through strategic partnerships, including sponsorships of the Nigerian Professional Football League, the foundation fosters community building and youth engagement.

One of its most notable healthcare initiatives is the launch of free mobile prostate cancer screening buses, providing essential medical services to underserved communities. The foundation also prioritizes economic empowerment, supporting young entrepreneurs and students by reducing financial barriers, particularly in STEM education.

Mmobuosi believes in the transformative power of collaboration and innovation, stating: "By investing in the next generation of leaders, we are building a brighter future for everyone." Through his foundation, he continues to drive sustainable change and create pathways to opportunity for countless individuals across Africa

Dozy’s efforts go beyond philanthropy and business; he is building lasting systems that empower African societies. From launching Africa Open University of Technology, an AI-driven institution democratizing STEM education, to establishing DM Capital Nigeria, a wealth management firm for African high-net-worth families, he is actively shaping the continent’s next chapter.
