About
Tingo Mobile

Tingo Mobile is a pioneering agri-fintech company dedicated to empowering smallholder farmers and agricultural enterprises across Africa. By integrating innovative technology with financial services, Tingo Mobile is bridging the gap between rural communities and the digital economy, fostering sustainable growth and economic inclusion.

Investing in Agricultural and Financial Innovation

Founded with the vision of revolutionizing Africa’s agricultural sector, Tingo Mobile has developed a suite of digital solutions that enhance productivity, streamline transactions, and provide access to crucial financial tools. The company’s ecosystem empowers farmers with real-time market insights, efficient payment systems, and direct access to buyers, ensuring a seamless agricultural value chain.

A Focus on Value Creation

Tingo Mobile is committed to creating lasting impact by offering tailored financial solutions that enhance business efficiency and market accessibility for farmers and agribusinesses. The company leverages digital technology to eliminate traditional barriers, providing users with the tools they need to increase their earnings and improve their livelihoods.

Innovative Solutions for a Digital Future

Recognizing the increasing need for financial inclusion and technological advancement in the agricultural sector, Tingo Mobile continues to innovate, developing user-friendly platforms that cater to the unique needs of smallholder farmers. These include:

  • TingoPay – A seamless bill payment platform that simplifies transactions.
  • Nwassa – A digital marketplace that connects farmers directly with buyers, ensuring fair prices and efficient trade.
  • Tingo POS – A robust payment processing system that offers competitive transaction rates for businesses.
  • Voucher Card – A convenient and secure shopping tool integrated with VISA for easy financial transactions.

Expanding Access to Financial Services

With a strong commitment to financial inclusion, Tingo Mobile provides smallholder farmers with the financial tools they need to expand their operations and improve their standard of living. Through its extensive digital infrastructure, the company ensures that farmers can seamlessly access credit, insurance, and savings products tailored to their unique needs.

The Future of Agri-Fintech in Africa

As the agricultural sector evolves, Tingo Mobile remains at the forefront of technological innovation, constantly refining its solutions to meet the changing demands of Africa’s growing economy. By integrating advanced financial technology with agricultural expertise, Tingo Mobile continues to unlock new opportunities for millions, driving sustainable development and economic prosperity across the continent.
