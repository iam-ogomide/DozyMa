About Dozy Mmobuosi

Dozy Mmobuosi is a globally recognized Nigerian entrepreneur, technology innovator, and philanthropist with a deep-rooted commitment to advancing Africa’s economic and social development. As the founder of several high-impact ventures spanning fintech, agriculture, AI, education, and infrastructure, Dozy’s mission is clear: to build a prosperous and self-reliant Africa through sustainable investments and inclusive innovation.

Learn About Mmobuosi

Early Life

Dozy Mmobuosi was born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. From an early age, he demonstrated a keen interest in technology, business, and leadership. Growing up in a dynamic and fast-paced environment, he developed a strong work ethic and an entrepreneurial mindset that would later define his career. His upbringing instilled in him a passion for solving real-world problems, particularly in Africa, where he saw opportunities to drive transformation through innovation and technology.

Education

Dozy pursued higher education at Ambrose Alli University in Nigeria, where he obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Political Science and later a Master of Science degree in Economics. Recognizing the importance of continuous learning, he further advanced his knowledge by completing the Advanced Management and Leadership Programme at Saïd Business School, University of Oxford. His academic background has played a significant role in shaping his strategic thinking and leadership abilities.

Career

Some of Dozy Mmobuosi's notable career ventures:

  • Tingo Mobile – Empowering Africa through fintech and mobile technology.
  • Tingo AI – Advancing AI-driven solutions across industries.
  • Tingo Foods – Strengthening Africa’s food production and supply chain.
  • Tingo Express – Enhancing logistics and e-commerce delivery.
  • Equity Group – Improving healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and mining in Africa.
  • 1472 FC – Developing young African football talent.
  • Africa Open Learning – Expanding access to digital education.
  • MLF Limited – Supporting business growth and investments.

Philanthropy

Dozy Mmobuosi is committed to giving back through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. In 2022, he launched the Dozy Mmobuosi Leadership Centre (DMLC) in Oxford to equip African leaders with transformative skills, reinforcing his vision for a thriving and self-sustaining Africa. He also founded 1472 FC, a Lagos-based football club focused on nurturing African talent and elevating Nigeria on the global sports map.

About Tingo Mobile

Tingo Mobile is a pioneering agri-fintech company dedicated to empowering smallholder farmers and agricultural enterprises across Africa. By integrating innovative technology with financial services, Tingo Mobile is bridging the gap between rural communities and the digital economy, fostering sustainable growth and economic inclusion.

With a strong commitment to financial inclusion, Tingo Mobile provides smallholder farmers with the financial tools they need to expand their operations and improve their standard of living. Through its extensive digital infrastructure, the company ensures that farmers can seamlessly access credit, insurance, and savings products tailored to their unique needs.

Philanthropy

Dozy Mmobuosi is deeply committed to philanthropy, focusing on education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. Through the Dozy Mmobuosi Foundation, he addresses critical social challenges across Africa, ensuring communities have access to opportunities for growth and development.

His foundation has launched scholarship programs for underprivileged students, funded medical outreach initiatives providing free healthcare services, and supported small businesses through funding and mentorship.

With a strong belief in giving back, Dozy Mmobuosi continues to expand his efforts, creating lasting impact and sustainable development across the continent.
